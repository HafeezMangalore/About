About US

Srinivas University Intends to expand its entrepreneurship and small business development activities through the Srinivas Innovation & Incubation Centre [SIIC]

Srinivas Innovation & Incubation Centre [SIIC]- is registered and approved under MSME and also section 8 of the Companies Act ,
which will provide end-to-end business development services to support the growth of Micro- Small-Medium Sector Enterprises
in Karnataka's coastal districts,as well as promote technopreneur ventures through high-quality professionals graduating from
the area's academic institutions.
